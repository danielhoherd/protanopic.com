One of the Anonymous slogans is "Expect us." The cops and the BART riders expected Anonymous tonight, so much so that most commuters went home early leaving mostly protestors and cops parading up and down Market street. I walked with them for a while to take photos, video, and stand up for freedom of speech.

There's something awesome about a man in a pin striped suit riding a bicycle, even without a mask and a megaphone.
