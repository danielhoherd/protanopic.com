Published: Sat 06 February 2010
By WarzauWynn

In Flickr 365, Life, Photography.

tags: 365 flickr Photography

WarzauWynn posted a photo:

This was my first morning waking up outside of San Francisco during my 32::AM project. Yesterday I moved out of my sublet with the girls and to hit the road to the south bay for a while. My first stop was Lila's house, which is always wonderful. This statue is in their front yard. Usually there are panoramic vies that would take your breath away, but this morning it was entirely fogy and wet. Truthfully, I was amazed that my lens didn't fog over while I was taking this shot, though it did shortly afterwards.
