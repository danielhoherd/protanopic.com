32::AM::268 - $75 shot

Published: Sat 09 October 2010
By WarzauWynn

In Flickr 365, Life, Photography.

tags: 365 Photography

WarzauWynn posted a photo:

The plane was on its descent, banking into a good view of my lens. I was framing up the shot when Tien tapped me on the shoulder and motioned... I looked to my left and there was a park ranger motioning for me to move. I nodded, looked back through my camera and took this shot. With that, the ranger got out of his vehicle and issued me a ticket, saying he would not have done so if I wouldn't have taken that last photograph. I thought that was kind of silly since there were about 10,000 people around for the air show violating all sorts of traffic laws, and there I was, color blind and parked in a red zone without knowing it.
