34::214 - Saint Thomas the Apostle Roman Catholic Church

2012-08-10 22:08:00-0700

WarzauWynn posted a photo:

Oh Christian God, please smite Muni with your mighty vengeance so that they may learn the error of their deceitful ways. Teach them that when a bus is scheduled to show up, it is a sin for one to not show up, but it is an abomination to miss two consecutive ones.

On a happier note, if either of the two 18's that were scheduled to show up had actually done so, I wouldn't have taken this photo. Also, if the river of the 31 hadn't completely ignored me when I motioned for her to open the door back up I wouldn't have gotten as good of a photo, because I took this right after she drove away and left Tien and I standing there in the cold windy fog.
