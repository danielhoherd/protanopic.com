32::AM::231 - Zero miles

Published: Mon 30 August 2010
By WarzauWynn

In Flickr 365, Life, Photography.

tags: 365 flickr Photography

WarzauWynn posted a photo:

Tien and I were walking to the bus today when we passed Golden Gate Cycles. There were several motorbikes parked outside, luring us in. So, we went in and looked around and rode off on a Yamaha Vino 125. I'd had my eye on them for a while, and $1000 off was a great deal, so what the heck? We scooted around town for a bit before picking up some Golden Boy Pizza, taking it to Savoy Tivoli and having pizza and a few drinks while listening to live jazz. Tien reacted badly to something she consumed, either the pizza or the half of a martini that she drank, and felt ill so we went home. It was a great day. :)
