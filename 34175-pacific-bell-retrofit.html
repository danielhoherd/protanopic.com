34::175 - Pacific Bell retrofit

Published: Mon 02 July 2012
By WarzauWynn

In Flickr 365, Life, Photography.

tags: 365 Photography

WarzauWynn posted a photo:

From what I understand, this is a seismic retrofit of the historic PacBell building. It's a beautiful building, I've always wanted to take photos inside of it. I'm glad they're repairing it so it can survive the next big quake. According to Wikipedia, this is the future home of Yelp.

Picked up this 18-55mm DX lens yesterday and was amazed at how well it worked on the D800 in FX mode. At 18mm it looks heavily vignetted like this, but from 22mm on it's much more gently stylized.

This shot had LR4's lens profile distortion correction applied, but not vignette removal.
