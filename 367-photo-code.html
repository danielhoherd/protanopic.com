In Flickr 365, Life, Photography.

tags: 365 Photography

WarzauWynn posted a photo:

Speaking of photo processing workflow, here's the only photo I took on Jan 16th: a snip of some of the code that pre-processes my photos when I plug in my cameras or phone or whatever various other photo devices to my computer. This helps me merge photos from all of my various cameras and devices into Lightroom in a way where I can verify uniqueness in the case of disaster recovery or when duplicates somehow creep in. Regarding duplicates, I use ZFS with deduplication on the backup side of things so even if duplicates interfere with my photo workflow, they don't take up much more room on my disks.
