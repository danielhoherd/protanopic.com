32::365 - Brief recline

  • By WarzauWynn

WarzauWynn posted a photo:

And thus we end my first 365 project with my wife reclining in throw-away furniture found on the streets of Chinatown in San Francisco. I plan to keep taking daily photos, and there's still no goal other than seeing where I was each day. Maybe this year I can remember to photograph the friends and acquaintances I encounter while we're talking instead of remembering to do so after we've already parted ways. I also hope to have this year span at least five countries, whereas I think this year was only four, but I don't yet have plans to visit any that I've not already been to.
