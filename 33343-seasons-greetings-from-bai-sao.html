33::343 - Seasons greetings from Bai Sao

Published: Tue 20 December 2011
By WarzauWynn

In Flickr 365, Life, Photography.

tags: 365 Photography

WarzauWynn posted a photo:

We rode down to Bai Sao today and found it almost unrecognizable as the placid paradise we discovered a year and a half ago. The wind was strong, the waves were large, the ocean was upset, and the beach had been reshaped into a short, steep drop directly down to the water. Apparently we had hit it at the perfect season last time. Still, it was gorgeous and we had a good time.

It was my joke this year that our white Christmas would involve white sandy beaches, but it was true!
